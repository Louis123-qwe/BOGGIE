Who We Are

About Boggie Energies

With a strong presence in Port Harcourt, Lagos, Warri, and the United Kingdom, we deliver industrial solutions that blend international best practices with deep local expertise.

We are recognised for reliability and excellence across Engineering Estimation, Project Controls, Information Management, Vessel Brokerage, Pipeline EPCI, Platform Fabrication, Procurement, and a full spectrum of construction support services.

Our team brings over 20 years of proven experience in the Construction and Upstream Oil & Gas sectors — delivering complex, high-value capital projects with strict control over cost, schedule, scope, safety, quality, and regulatory compliance.

What We Do

Core Services

From concept to commissioning — we provide the full spectrum of services that energy projects demand.

Vessel Brokerage

We provide onshore and offshore construction vessel brokerage services for global energy infrastructure, sourcing the right marine assets for every project scope and environment.

Pipeline EPCI

We handle the full Engineering, Procurement, Construction, and Installation of carbon steel, flexible, and unbonded flexible pipelines — alongside shop fabrication of structures and appurtenances and a broad range of related construction services.

OEM Representation

We promote, market, and support manufacturers' products within designated regions — bridging OEMs and end clients with technical advisory, sales support, and after-sales assistance while upholding manufacturer standards.

Engineering Estimation & Project Controls

We bring extensive expertise in technical and commercial estimating — covering project cost evaluation, resource allocation, scheduling, progress monitoring, risk assessment, and a clear financial roadmap from planning through full execution.

Tender & Bid Documentation Training

We deliver targeted training for upstream and downstream Oil & Gas companies, equipping teams with the practical knowledge to prepare compliant, competitive, and technically sound tender submissions that win.
